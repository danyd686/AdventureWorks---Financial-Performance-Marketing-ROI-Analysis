# 📈 AdventureWorks - Financial Performance & Marketing ROI Analysis
Project Overview
## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes AdventureWorks' financial performance across six international markets to evaluate profitability, marketing efficiency, and return on investment (ROI).

The analysis focuses on identifying high-performing markets, detecting inefficiencies in marketing spending, and generating strategic recommendations to optimize resource allocation and reduce business dependency on a limited number of markets.

The project was developed using SQL and Google Sheets as part of the TripleTen Data Analytics Bootcamp.
Business Problem
## 🎯 Business Problem

The company invests heavily in marketing campaigns across multiple international markets, but not all countries generate the same return.

The objective of this analysis was to answer the following questions:

- Which markets generate the highest revenue and profitability?
- Which countries have inefficient marketing investments?
- How dependent is the business on specific markets?
- What opportunities exist to improve future growth strategies?

Key Findings
## 📈 Key Findings

### 🇺🇸 United States leads overall performance

- Highest revenue: $3.35M
- Highest gross profit: $1.45M
- Highest ROI: 75.75%

This indicates exceptional marketing efficiency and strong market demand.

---

### 🇪🇺 European markets show low marketing efficiency

United Kingdom, Germany and France receive marketing investments above $2.2M but generate ROI values between 17% and 22%.

This suggests higher customer acquisition costs or less effective marketing strategies.

---

### 🇨🇦 Canada has hidden growth potential

Canada has:

- Lowest revenue
- Lowest ROI (17.43%)
- Highest profit margin (44.76%)

This indicates efficient operational performance but insufficient demand generation.
Business Recommendations

Esta sección es la más importante.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Replicate successful strategies from the United States

The U.S. market demonstrates superior marketing efficiency and customer acquisition performance.

Its marketing strategies could be adapted to underperforming markets.

---

### 2. Increase growth efforts in Canada

Canada already operates efficiently.

Improving demand generation and customer acquisition could significantly increase overall profitability.

---

### 3. Reduce dependency on two major markets

The United States and Australia account for 59.88% of total business profit.

Diversifying growth across additional markets would reduce commercial risk.

---

### 4. Optimize European marketing campaigns

Future growth should prioritize campaign optimization rather than increasing marketing spending.
